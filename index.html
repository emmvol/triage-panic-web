TRIAGE PANIC is a diagnostic and survival simulation developed as a personal project. This document outlines the protocols regarding the handling of user data within the digital environment of the Game.

CORE PROTOCOL: Triage Panic is designed as a local-first experience. We prioritize user sovereignty over data.

01. DATA COLLECTION & STORAGE

We do NOT collect, harvest, or transmit any personally identifiable information (PII). All neural synchronization data, including:

  • Financial assets (VitaCredits)
  • Biometric status (Health, Sanity, Stress)
  • Inventory logs (Fridge & Equipment)

Is stored exclusively on your local hardware. We have no access to your save files.

02. THIRD-PARTY ARCHITECTURE

The Game is constructed using the Flutter and Flame frameworks. No external telemetry, surveillance SDKs, or advertising tracking modules are integrated into the system.

03. PROTECTION OF MINORS

Our protocols strictly adhere to international privacy standards. Since no data is collected, we do not possess any information from users, including children under the age of 13. If you believe a breach has occurred, please initiate contact immediately.

04. PROTOCOL UPDATES

This Privacy Policy may be recalibrated to align with new regulatory requirements. Users are advised to monitor this terminal periodically for version increments.
