Privacy Policy

System: VitaStabilizer_OS // Studio: Emmvol Studios // Status: SECURE

This Privacy Policy is provided by Emmvol Studios as the developer of TRIAGE PANIC. This document outlines the protocols for user data management within the Game's digital environment.

CORE PROTOCOL: Data is handled locally by default. External modules are utilized for connectivity, analytics, and network maintenance subsidies.

01. LOCAL DATA STORAGE

By default, all progress (VitaCredits, Stats, Inventory) is stored locally on your hardware. This data remains on your device unless you utilize cloud synchronization services.

02. DATA COLLECTION & EXTERNAL SERVICES

To provide advanced functionality and ensure system stability, the Game interfaces with Google Play infrastructure. The following data is collected and transmitted to Google Firebase and Google Play Services:

  • User Identifiers: Digital IDs to manage progress, leaderboards, and achievements.
  • Approximate Location: IP-based location for regional analytics and ad optimization.
  • App Interactions: Data regarding how you navigate the hospital menus.
  • Diagnostics: Crash logs and performance reports to fix system bugs.

Refer to Google's Privacy Policy and Firebase Security for details.

03. ADVERTISING PROTOCOLS

To subsidize the development of the Lifeline Care Network, we utilize Google Mobile Ads (AdMob). This service collects:

  • Advertising ID: To provide relevant advertisements.
  • Interaction Logs: Technical data regarding your interaction with ads.

Data is shared with third-party advertising partners. You may reset your Advertising ID within your device's Google Settings menu.

04. DATA DELETION & RIGHTS

Users have the right to request the deletion of their data. To request a permanent wipe of your records from our synchronized services, please contact us at emmvol.dev@gmail.com.

05. PROTECTION OF MINORS

Our protocols adhere to COPPA/GDPR safety standards. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. If you believe a neural link breach has occurred, contact us immediately.

06. UPDATES & CONTACT

This policy may be recalibrated for legal compliance. For inquiries, contact the lead developer at:
