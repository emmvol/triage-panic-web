Privacy Policy

TRIAGE PANIC is a survival simulation. This document outlines the protocols for user data management within the Game's digital environment.

CORE PROTOCOL: Neural data is handled locally by default. External synchronization and advertisement modules are utilized for connectivity and network maintenance subsidies.

01. LOCAL DATA STORAGE

By default, all progress (VitaCredits, Stats, Inventory) is stored locally on your hardware. We do not have access to this information unless you utilize cloud synchronization services.

02. EXTERNAL SERVICES & MULTIPLAYER

To provide advanced functionality, the Game may interface with Google Play infrastructure:

  • Google Play Games: Used for authentication, leaderboards, and achievements.
  • Firebase (Google): Utilized for real-time multiplayer synchronization and secure cloud storage.

These services may collect technical identifiers (such as User IDs) to ensure stable network operations. Refer to Google's Privacy Policy for details.

03. ADVERTISING PROTOCOLS

To subsidize the development of the Lifeline Care Network, we utilize the Google Mobile Ads SDK (AdMob). This service may collect and use data including:

  • Advertising ID: To provide personalized or contextual advertisements.
  • Interaction Logs: Technical data regarding device model and interaction with ads.

Users may recalibrate their ad personalization settings within their Android device's Google Settings menu.

04. DATA PROTECTION

We do NOT sell or trade your data to external corporate entities. Project Triage Panic does not use invasive surveillance SDKs or third-party unauthorized trackers.

05. PROTECTION OF MINORS

Our protocols adhere to COPPA/GDPR safety standards. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. If you believe a neural link breach has occurred, contact us immediately.
