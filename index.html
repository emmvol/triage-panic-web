This Privacy Policy is provided by Emmvol Studios as the developer of TRIAGE PANIC. This document outlines the protocols for user data management.

CORE PROTOCOL: Data is handled locally by default. External modules are utilized for connectivity, analytics, and network maintenance subsidies.

01. LOCAL DATA STORAGE

By default, all progress (VitaCredits, Stats, Inventory) is stored locally on your hardware. This data remains on your device unless you utilize cloud synchronization services.

02. DATA COLLECTION & EXTERNAL SERVICES

The Game interfaces with Google Play infrastructure. The following data is collected and transmitted to Google Firebase and Google Play Services:

We utilize Google Mobile Ads (AdMob). This service collects your Advertising ID and Interaction Logs to provide relevant advertisements and subsidize development. You may reset your Advertising ID in your device settings.
